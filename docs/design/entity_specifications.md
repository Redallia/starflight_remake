# Entity Specifications
Different game entities and details about each one

## Crew Entity
Contains:
- name
- species
- assigned ship role(s)
- skills, and training level
- health

Crew entities are figures assigned to the player ship. They can be created and assigned to their role in Starport.
Decesased crew members abandon their assigned role, and show up as memorial in Starport.

## Ship Entity
Contains:
- name
- ship class
- crew roster, if player's ship
- faction/species
- equipment
    - weapons
    - armor
    - shields
    - additional technology or artifacts
- coordinate location in either hyperspace, or local space
- cargo
- fuel level
